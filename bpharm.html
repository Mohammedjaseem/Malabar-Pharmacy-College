BPharm Program

Bachelor of Pharmacy

The Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) program at Malabar College of Pharmacy is a four-year undergraduate course designed to provide students with a comprehensive foundation in pharmacy practice, pharmaceutical sciences, and patient care — following the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) curriculum.
