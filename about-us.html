About Malabar College of Pharmacy

Malabar College of Pharmacy is dedicated to excellence in pharmacy education, research, and community service. Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and robust curriculum are designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for a successful career in pharmacy.

Comprehensive Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to foster expertise and proficiency in pharmaceutical sciences, preparing students for diverse career paths.

Global Opportunities

We provide our students with international exposure and opportunities to ensure they gain a global perspective in pharmacy practices.
