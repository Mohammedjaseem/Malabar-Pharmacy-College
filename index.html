About Malabar College of Pharmacy

Malabar College of Pharmacy is dedicated to excellence in pharmacy education, research, and community service. Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and robust curriculum are designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for a successful career in pharmacy.

Comprehensive Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to foster expertise and proficiency in pharmaceutical sciences, preparing students for diverse career paths.

Global Opportunities

We provide our students with international exposure and opportunities to ensure they gain a global perspective in pharmacy practices.

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B-Pharm)

Department

Browse Our Pharmacy Departments

Explore our diverse pharmacy departments and their contributions to the field.

Pharmaceutical Chemistry

Focuses on drug discovery and development with hands-on lab experience in organic and medicinal chemistry.

Pharmaceutics

Emphasizes drug formulation, biopharmaceutics, and delivery systems with extensive lab training.

Pharmacology

Studies drug actions and interactions, focusing on pharmacodynamics, pharmacokinetics, and toxicology.

Pharmacognosy

Explores medicinal plants and natural products, focusing on bioactive compound identification and extraction.

Pharmacy Practice

Covers clinical and community pharmacy practice, including patient counseling and medication management.
