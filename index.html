About Malabar Pharmacy College

Nurturing Future Pharmacists With Excellence.

Malabar Pharmacy College has been a cornerstone in pharmacy education for over 30 years, blending tradition with innovation to provide a superior learning environment. Our commitment to excellence ensures that our students are equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to make significant contributions to the healthcare industry.

Comprehensive Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to foster expertise and proficiency in pharmaceutical sciences, preparing students for diverse career paths.

Global Opportunities

We provide our students with international exposure and opportunities to ensure they gain a global perspective in pharmacy practices.
