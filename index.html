Our Programs

Academic Programs

Explore our pharmacy programs designed to prepare students for meaningful careers in healthcare and pharmaceutical sciences.

B.Pharm

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm)

A comprehensive 4-year program covering pharmacology, pharmaceutical chemistry, pharmacognosy, and pharmacy practice, preparing students for diverse career paths in the pharmaceutical industry.

4 Years
60 Seats
10 Modules
D.Pharm

Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm)

A 2-year diploma program that provides foundational knowledge in pharmaceutical sciences, covering drug dispensing, formulation, clinical pharmacy, and healthcare delivery systems.

2 Years
60 Seats
8 Modules
